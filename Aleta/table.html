Alappuzha

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is a picturesque district in the southern Indian state of Kerala. Renowned for its intricate network of backwaters, lagoons, and canals, Alappuzha is often referred to as the "Venice of the East. " The region is celebrated for its serene landscapes, houseboat cruises, and vibrant cultural heritage.

Kozhikode

Kozhikode, also known as Calicut, is a vibrant city located in the state of Kerala, India. Steeped in history and cultural significance, Kozhikode has been a prominent trading hub for centuries, with a rich maritime heritage. The city was a pivotal center for the spice trade during the medieval period, attracting traders from various parts of the world.

Thekkady

Thekkady is a captivating destination situated in the Idukki district of Kerala, India. Renowned for its lush forests, wildlife sanctuaries, and spice plantations, Thekkady is a haven for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
